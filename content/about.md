# About me

I am Sergio, a software developer who really likes learning by building things and understanding how they work from the inside.

Most of what I spend time on is backend development, infrastructure, Linux, Kubernetes, and homelab projects. I like working on systems that are practical, simple, and easy to reason about.

This blog is mostly a place for me to write about what I am studying, what I am building, and the things I learn while working on personal projects. Sometimes it is a tutorial, sometimes it is just a note for my future self.

I also like keeping my own infrastructure running, automating repetitive work, and building tools that make my setup easier to maintain.

If something here helps you or gives you an idea, then that is already enough for me.
